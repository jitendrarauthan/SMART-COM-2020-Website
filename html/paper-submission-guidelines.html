Paper Submission Guidelines

Instruction to Authors

Full paper in format should be submitted through on-line paper submission process (Easy chair submission system)

Click Here

Manuscripts for SMART COM 2020 will ONLY be accepted in electronic format through EasyChair online submission system. Please do not send hard copy of your paper. If you are unable to submit your contribution electronically, please contact us.

Click the links below to download

  • US letter (DOC, 62 KB) Updated May 2018
  • A4 (DOC, 56 KB) Updated May 2018
  • LaTeX Template Instructions (PDF, 63 KB) [Be sure to use the template's conference mode.]
  • Template (ZIP, 700 KB) Updated October 2019
  • LaTeX Bibliography Files (ZIP, 309 KB)
  • Submit your manuscript either in PDF or in Microsoft Word format.
  • Authors should only submit originally written, unpublished work to SMART COM 2020
  • Submissions should strictly follow the recommended conference template.
  • All references must follow the format of referencing
  • To facilitate the paper’s double-blind peer review process, authors should not mention name and affiliation at the top as per template and make efforts to ensure that information about the authors identities/name/affiliation do not appear anywhere in the text-body of the paper. (for the Initial submission)
  • Authors will be considered as mentioned in easychair
  • Please limit your paper within 6 pages, and the size of your PDF file less than 2MB, files larger than 2MB will not be accepted for upload.
  • Authors wishing to submit an over-length manuscript may do so. There will be an over-length page fee of Rs. 2,000/page for Indian participant and $125 per page for foreign participant and limited to an additional 2 pages (maximum page submission is eight (8) pages). This payment is required at the time of registration.
  • All fonts must be embedded in the file.
  • Authors can't be added or removed at later stage.
  • Fonts that require non-English language support are not allowed.
  • The document should not have any password protection.
  • Do not put your own page numbers on the manuscript.
